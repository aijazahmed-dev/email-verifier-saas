What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device computer, tablet, or mobile when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide information to the site owner, and enhance your browsing experience. Cookies do not harm your device and are not programs or viruses.

There are different types of cookies, and their purpose can vary. Some cookies are essential for the website to function properly, while others help us improve your experience or analyze website performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies

    These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website. They enable core features such as page navigation, security, and access to secure areas of the website. Without these cookies, our website may not function properly.

  2. Performance Cookies

    Performance cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most frequently, how long users stay on a page, and if they encounter any errors. These cookies help us improve the performance and user experience of our website.

  3. Functional Cookies

    Functional cookies allow our website to remember choices you make, such as your language preference, login details, or region. These cookies help enhance your experience by providing personalized features and content.

  4. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

    These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests. They may also limit the number of times you see an ad and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. These cookies may be placed by third-party advertising networks.

  5. Analytics Cookies

    Analytics cookies allow us to understand how users interact with our website. They help us track website traffic, visitor behavior, and overall engagement. We use this information to improve our website and services.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to manage and control cookies. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all cookies
  • Block specific types of cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session

Please note that if you choose to disable essential cookies, some features of our website may not function correctly. Disabling cookies may also affect your browsing experience and limit access to certain features.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Here's how you can manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data → Manage settings
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage Preferences
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Safari (Mac): Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

For more detailed instructions, please visit your browser's official support page.
